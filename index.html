Our Story

ABOUT SRIJANX

SrijanX was born from a simple obsession: that the things we love most — the characters that shaped us, the moments that moved us, the idols that inspired us — deserve to exist in the real world.

Every piece we create is a bridge between a screen and a shelf. We design, print, paint, and finish each miniature by hand — giving it weight, texture, and life that no digital image can match.

From anime figures and chibi collectibles to wedding keepsakes and custom fan gifts — if you can dream it, we can build it. Powered by precision 3D printing, painted with care, delivered with pride.

Got Questions?

FAQ

What are the prints made of? +
All prints are made using FDM 3D printing with PLA/PETG filament. They are durable, lightweight and can be painted in any color.
Do the prints come painted? +
By default prints are colorless (single filament color). You can choose to: get them painted by us (additional cost), specify colors on WhatsApp, or paint them yourself.
What is the maximum print size? +
Maximum print size is 10.08 × 10.08 × 10.08 inches (256mm³). For larger items, we can print in multiple parts and assemble. Let us know your requirements on WhatsApp.
How is pricing decided? +
Pricing depends on the model complexity, size, color requirements and finishing. We discuss and confirm the final price on WhatsApp before printing — no surprises.
Can I request a fully custom design? +
Yes! We do fully custom work — wedding figurines, portraits, fan gifts, anything. Share a reference image on WhatsApp and we'll work it out together.
How do I pay? +
Currently cash on delivery or in-person payment only. We'll confirm payment details on WhatsApp before dispatch.
How long does an order take? +
Typical turnaround is 3-7 days depending on complexity, size and painting requirements. We'll give you an accurate timeline on WhatsApp after confirming your order.
Do you ship or only deliver locally? +
Currently we are based in Bengaluru and offer local delivery. Shipping options will be available soon — reach out on WhatsApp to discuss.
